What's this page about?

This is a personal page dedicated only to One Piece, as a fan of this work, I want to collect all the information and make it more attractive to new people who want to discover this story, as it can be overwhelming due to the years that the manga has been in development, the anime is still on air, and recently began the live action on netflix, there are many ways to discover this world, and all are correct, different, and independently enjoyable. Welcome nakama* to the longest pirate adventure in history!
